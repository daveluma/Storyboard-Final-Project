Storyboard encourages writers to share their ideas, drawings/images, & prose amongst users. Other users will then be able to like and comment on the post to provide feedback through beta-reading each other's work or to show appreciation. Users who enjoyed the work of a particular author may also visit their profile and see all the posts from that user. Lastly, challenges can be created by users which can be distinguished by a yellow genre tag on the home screen. Users are able to create their own challenges, for example asking other writers to continue a story or analyzing the meaning behind a post. Then other users can respond in the comments to the challenge with their answer. A user will know they are on a challenge post by the bright yellow title unlike the usual white one.
